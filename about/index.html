About me

I'm Leo, a student at the United World College of Southeast Asia in Singapore - expected to graduate 2022. I've previously lived in Yongin, South Korea then Beijing, China before coming to Singapore. Thanks to that, I am fluent in English and Korean, and moderately proficient in Mandarin Chinese (HSK 5 level). I also love computers, music, and travelling.

I love taking on new exciting projects and meeting other people with similar interests. My contact details are at the contact me section of this website - don't hesitate to leave me an email or a DM if you want to talk!
