UWCOI

TECHNICAL COMMITTEE
2020

United World College Olympiad in Informatics, or UWCOI for short, is an Online Mirror of the selection test for the UWCSEA NOI (National Olympiad in Informatics) team. As it was a large international contest with over 1400 participants, our roles included answering problem clarifications in real time and creating robust testdata for our tasks, among other logistical challenges.
